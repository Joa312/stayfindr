📋 Terms of Service & Privacy

🏨 Hotel Booking

STAYFINDR connects you directly to Booking.com for hotel reservations. All bookings are processed by Booking.com under their terms and conditions.

We do not handle payments or reservations directly. Your booking contract is with the hotel and Booking.com.

🔒 Privacy & Data

We respect your privacy and comply with GDPR regulations. Your search data is used only to provide hotel results and is not stored permanently.

No personal information is collected without your consent. Booking data is handled by Booking.com according to their privacy policy.

💰 Pricing & Accuracy

All prices are provided in real-time by Booking.com and may change. Final prices are confirmed during the booking process on Booking.com.

STAYFINDR is not responsible for price discrepancies or booking issues. Please verify all details on Booking.com before confirming your reservation.

🌍 Service Availability

Our service covers 29 major European cities. Availability depends on Booking.com's inventory and real-time data feeds.

Service may be temporarily unavailable due to maintenance or technical issues. We strive for 99% uptime but cannot guarantee uninterrupted service.
